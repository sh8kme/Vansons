Who We Are

Experienced Pros

Van & Sons is your home renovation specialist. Locally owned and operated, we are experienced general contractors with the know-how to renovate your home or business. With over 25 years of experience building even the most ambitious of renovation projects we can bring our skills and dependability to your next project.

Professional

We bring great attention to detail along with a dependable team that consistently delivers exceptional craftsmanship. We have built a reputation of superior quality and are committed to servicing western Ontario's home and business renovation needs.

Energy Efficient

We specialize in green renovations using sustainably sourced materials, post-consumer or post-industrial recycled content, or resource-efficient products to reduce energy usage.

Cost Conscious

We reliably and consistently bring projects in under budget. Our clients have piece of mind that there won't be any cost surprises. What we quote is what you pay ...not a cent more.

Warrantied

We offer an iron-clad 3 year warranty on all of our renovation work. If you're not satisfied, we will return until you are. We stake our reputation on our work.
